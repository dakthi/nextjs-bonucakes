## Current State Assessment

5. How do customers currently find you online?
6. What social media platforms are you most active on?

### Content Inventory

4. What content needs editing or updating?

### Current Workflow

2. How do customers register for workshops?

4. What tools/platforms are you using now? (email, booking, social media, etc.)

## Vision & Strategy

### Brand & Positioning

1. Who is your ideal customer?
2. What transformation do you want to provide for them?
3. Beyond cooking skills, what are you really teaching?
4. How is Bonucakes different from other cooking schools/restaurants?
5. What do you want people to feel when they experience Bonucakes?

### Business Goals

1. What are your top 3 business goals for the next year?
2. Is the focus more on workshops, restaurant operations, or both?
3. Do you want to grow the online/digital side of the business?
4. Are you planning to offer online courses or just in-person workshops?

### Content Strategy

4. Will content be in Vietnamese, English, or bilingual?
5. Who will create content - just you, or do you have a team?

## Technical Requirements

### Content Management

1. Do you want to update the website yourself, or need ongoing support?
2. How comfortable are you with technology? (CMS, scheduling posts, etc.)
3. Would you prefer a simple interface or more control/flexibility?
4. Do you need the ability to schedule content in advance?

### Video & Media

1. Where are your videos currently hosted? (YouTube, Vimeo, other)
2. Do you want to keep using that platform or switch?
3. How much video content do you have? (storage considerations)
4. Do you want videos embedded or do you have privacy concerns?
5. Who edits your videos?

### Workshop & Event Management

1. How many workshops do you typically run per month?
2. Are workshops free or paid?
3. If paid, how much do you charge?
4. How do you want to handle registrations? (form, payment, capacity limits)
5. Do you send reminder emails to attendees?
6. Do you provide recordings or materials after workshops?
7. Do you want a calendar view of upcoming events?

### Email & Communication

1. Do you currently have an email list? How many subscribers?
2. What email platform are you using? (Mailchimp, ConvertKit, etc.)
3. How often do you send newsletters?
4. What do you typically include in newsletters?
5. Do you want to migrate your existing list or start fresh?
6. Do you want automated emails? (welcome series, workshop reminders, etc.)

### Community Features

1. Do you want a members-only area on the website?
2. What would members get access to? (exclusive recipes, videos, forum, etc.)
3. Do you want a discussion forum or community space?
4. Should members be able to comment on recipes/posts?
5. Do you want to track student progress or achievements?
6. Any subscription or membership fee?

### E-commerce & Payments

1. Are you selling anything? (courses, consultations, merchandise, etc.)
2. If yes, what payment methods do you want to accept?
3. Do you have a Stripe or PayPal account?
4. Do you need invoicing functionality?
5. Any tax considerations for online sales?

### Integrations

1. What tools do you currently use daily? (Google Calendar, social media schedulers, etc.)
2. Which of these need to connect to your website?
3. Do you use a CRM system?
4. Any accounting software we need to integrate with?
5. Do you use WhatsApp or Telegram for customer communication?

## Technical Decisions to Make Together

### Tech Stack (explain options)

**Content Management:**
- Option A: Sanity.io (headless CMS - more flexible)
- Option B: Built-in MDX files (simpler, developer-managed)
- Option C: Custom admin panel (most control)

**Video Hosting:**
- Option A: Continue with YouTube/Vimeo (free, but ads/branding)
- Option B: Self-hosted with Cloudinary or Mux (more control, costs money)
- Option C: Hybrid (free videos on YouTube, premium on paid platform)

**Email Platform:**
- Option A: Resend (developer-friendly, modern)
- Option B: Keep existing platform and integrate
- Option C: ConvertKit (creator-focused)
- Option D: Mailchimp (familiar, feature-rich)

**Authentication (for members area):**
- Option A: NextAuth.js (free, integrated)
- Option B: Clerk (beautiful UI, easier to set up)
- Option C: No members area initially, add later

**Payments (if needed):**
- Option A: Stripe (industry standard, powerful)
- Option B: PayPal (familiar to customers)
- Option C: Both
